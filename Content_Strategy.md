To build a high-impact personal brand that demonstrates hands-on technical proficiency, analytical thinking, and a proactive mindset. The ultimate objective is to leverage this content to secure a SOC Tier 1 role and build the foundation for a highly lucrative career in cybersecurity and OSINT.

**Cadence:** Bi-weekly (1 post every 2 weeks)
**Goal:** Maintain consistent output while protecting core study time for certifications (A+, Network+, Security+) and lab training.

* **Week 1: The Capture Phase**

## 🛠️ Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

### Video Production (YouTube)
* **Hook:** First 15 seconds must establish the problem and the technical solution.
* **Pacing:** Edit like a gameplay montage. Remove all dead air. Zoom in on terminal commands and critical UI elements.
* **Call to Action (CTA):** Drive viewers to the GitHub repo for documentation and LinkedIn for networking.

### Graphic Design (LinkedIn Carousels)
* **Style:** Clean, digital magazine aesthetic. High contrast, highly readable typography.
* **Structure:** * Slide 1: Hook/Title.
    * Slide 2-3: The problem and the tool.
    * Slide 4: The technical execution (screenshots).
    * Slide 5: Takeaways and CTA.
